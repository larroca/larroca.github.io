About Me

I am an evolutionary biologist integrating -omics and computer vison tools to ask fundamental questions related to the interplay between animal behavior and brain evolution. I am currently a Researcher at the Department of Ecology and Genetics in Uppsala University, funded by the Birgitta Sintring Foundation and the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences.

I completed my PhD in Ethology at Stockholm University working in Niclas Kolm’s group. Next, I have taken postdoctoral positions at University College London and University of British Columbia (Judith Mank's lab), and Uppsala Unversity (Vaishali Katju’s lab)
